Boston's Freedom Festival

Join us in Boston for a Bitcoin-only festival celebrating the revolutionary power of Bitcoin. Enjoy delicious treats from food trucks that accept sats and a authentic Texas BBQ if you opt for the Cattlemen's Feast. Immerse yourself in informative talks and engaging discussions led by prominent Bitcoin speakers, while enjoying live music performances by local and international artists. Explore a wide variety of Bitcoin merchandise, from customized t-shirts to hardware wallets, and vintage clothing. Compete in 3 v 3 basketball competitions for the chance to earn some sats. Take in the beautiful New England summer as the backdrop to the festival, and seize the opportunity to explore the historic city of Boston, home to some of the most iconic landmarks of American history. The MassAdoption Freedom Festival is a chance to Connect with other Bitcoin enthusiasts, plebs, and hodlers alike, to share stories, ideas, and insights about the revolutionary power of Bitcoin while enjoying a memorable weekend filled with good times and great company.

Live Music Performances By

Captain Youth

Captain Youth is an independent singer/songwriter from Michigan. He is a freedom lover and bitcoin enthusiast. His music can be described as heartfelt stories that take your mind through a journey of deep introspection, while contrasted with compelling beats and lyrics that keep listeners coming back for more.

The Higher Low

The Higher Low (THL) is the acoustic solo project of the Finnish rock musician Rami K, the lead singer of the melodic punk rock pioneer band Fumble - founded in 1992. THL makes punchy songs about the Bitcoin freedom revolution by blending energetic punk rock melodies with campfire-style acoustic strumming. THL released its debut album, "Node To Freedom," in January 2023.

Orange Baron

Bandele El aka "The Orange Baron" lives a life of 100% positive energy. His mission is to reach billions of people by spreading awareness of bitcoin through great music and good vibes. He's performed his song, Bitcoin Ballin, all over the world including at conferences like Bitcoin Miami, Accra Ghana, and El Zonte El Salvador.
